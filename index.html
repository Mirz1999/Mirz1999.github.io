WHY Hardworking with a passion for creation, problem

My young self has always been passionate about technology. I started playing video games on my old PC when I was 9, and loved to disassemble it and building it back up. Computer Science was always the way to go for me, logical thinking is something I've always been drawn to. I also fell in love with drawing from a very young age and had a way with colors and design. Now, I am passionate about both Web and Game Development, using my logical fast thinking, but also my creativity and imagination, to be as productive as possible. Nice to meet you !
