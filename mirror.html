For my final year of Computer Science project, I decided to build a SmartMirror with the help of two of my classmates. A Smart Mirror is a two-way mirror with an inbuilt display behind the glass. The display can show anything you want on the mirror’s surface, with the glass still effective as a mirror.

  • RaspberryPi powered.
  • Using MagicMirror (Open source, Community driven, Modular smart mirror platform)
  • Features: Current time, Weather, News feed, Traffic map (Using GoogleMaps API), Phone’s notifications (Using Push Bullet), Alexa voice control, Remote Controlled from your phone.
